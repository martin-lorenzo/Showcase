How to Play

  1. Choose a tile from the pair directly in front of you. If correct, you may proceed to the next pair. If incorrect, you lose a life, and restart at the beginning.
  2. Continue making choices through all 5 pairs of tiles, remembering previously chosen incorrect tiles to avoid repeating mistakes.
  3. Win by reaching the "Finish" tile without losing all 3 lives. Good luck and enjoy the game!
